Project Caring & Sharing is a private, non-profit 501 © (3) organization. Since 1981, Project Caring & Sharing has provided emergency food banks, clothing, and personal hygiene items giveaways, health fairs, back-to-school giveaways, holiday food baskets and holiday toys giveaways to single heads of households, senior citizens and the working poor in the Pomona Valley, San Gabriel Valley and the Inland Empire.
