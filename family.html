Project Caring and Sharing offers family support programs for different aspects of families and needs. The available programs are shown on the left and click on each one for more information.

Back-to-School Giveaway Program:

Children are provided with back-to-school supplies for the upcoming school year.

Community Health and Resource Fair:

Free annual health fairs are offered each year to low-income and needy families and individuals.

Community organizations and agencies are highlighted.

Food and Clothing Distribution Program:

Our Food and Clothing Distribution Program provides overflowing emergency food boxes which are distributed to low-income and single heads of household families, senior citizens and needy individuals as needed

Family Support Classes:

A series of on-going family support classes, including parenting, anger management, domestic violence, budgeting, tenant rights and responsibilities classes and life skills management training are offered by a variety of community organizations and advocates.

Anger Management Classes:

A 26 to 52 week class, depending on the needs of the individual. The classes are facilitated by certified counselors.

Motherhood Classes:

A 26 week class that supports and prepares mothers looking for ways to help their children cope with the fears and uncertainties that are a natural part of our world and its many crisis situations. The groups are taught under two different categories: married and single mothers. The classes are facilitated by certified counselors.

Project Fatherhood Classes:

A 26 week class where fathers can deal with and heal unresolved issues that disrupt their relationships with their children and others. The Fatherhood curriculum is designed to help fathers engage and support their children and simply be better fathers.

Strengthening Families Classes:

This is a 14 session, science based parenting skills, children's life skills, and family life skills training program specifically designed for high-risk families.

The Truth about Drugs Classes:

This is a 18 session program specifically designed to reach young people with effective, fact-based drug education before they start experimenting with drugs.

Holiday Giveaway Programs:

We have an annual holiday (Thanksgiving) food giveaway, as well as an annual toys (Christmas) giveaway.
